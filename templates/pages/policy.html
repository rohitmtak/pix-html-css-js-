Privacy Policy

By visiting this Website, you agree to be bound by the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree please refrain from using our Website.

Privacy Philosophies

We at PIX follow certain privacy principles in our conduct in the collection, use, release and security of data both online and offline :

  • Providing you with NOTICE of our data practices;
  • Giving you with CHOICES about how your data will be used;
  • Providing you with the opportunity to UPDATE or CORRECT your personal data;
  • Using data SECURITY safeguards;
  • Limiting the SHARING of your data;
  • Committing to COMPLYING with applicable privacy requirements;
  • Providing you with means to CONTACT US about privacy-related issues.

Privacy Protection

Our privacy policy is simple: any data you give us stays with us.

We respect your privacy and commit to ensure that we treat all data provided by you with the highest diligence and integrity. This also and especially includes the cooperation with partners and third parties. We however do not accept responsibility for third parties, unless stated otherwise.

When you use our Website, we collect and store your personal data which is provided by you from time to time. Our primary goal in doing so is to provide you a safe, efficient, smooth and customized experience. This allows us to provide services and features that most likely meet your needs, and to customize our Website to make your experience safer and easier. More importantly, while doing so we collect personal data from you that we consider necessary for fulfilling contractual relationship between PIX and you.

We secure our Website and other systems through technical and organizational measures against loss, destruction, unauthorized access, modification and distribution of your data. You agree that despite regular controls, a complete protection against all dangers is not possible.

In general, you can browse the Website without telling us who you are or revealing any personal data about yourself. Once you give us your personal data, you are not anonymous to us.

Limited Data Access

Within our organization, your personal data is accessible to only a limited number of employees with special access privileges. Although we may, from time to time, compile general demographic data based on your order, this data is shared within our organization only and has no identifiable personal data associated with it.

Data Collected

To enable you to place an order on our Website, we need to have the following basic data about you : first and last name, email address, mailing address, telephone numbers, date of birth, anniversary date, gender, size, address, phone number, contact e-mail address and if you transact business with us, your billing address and your limited financial data. To ensure your Services are enhanced, PIX may use and collect your location.

Apart from this, our systems gather certain details about (i) your device’s internet connection like your IP address when you visit our site, and its geographic location; (ii) browser type and version, language, time zone setting, browser plug-in types & version, OS and platform, domain names, access times, referring website addresses; (iii) your search terms or interactive behavior via our Website; and (iv) your purchasing behavior. This data does not identify you personally. We may use this data in aggregate form for internal business purposes, such as generating statistics and developing marketing plans and to customize our site as per your interest.

We do not allow any unauthorized person or organization be it other members, visitors, and anyone not in our organization to use any data collected from you.

Note : Our privacy policy is subject to change at any time without notice. Unless stated otherwise, our current Privacy Policy applies to all data that we have about you and your account.

Data Modification

You can modify your personal details or unsubscribe the receipt of promotional emails we send you by accessing the Edit Profile option at any time from our Website.

You have the right to receive free data about your stored data at any time as well as the right to correct, lock or delete this data. In such a case, however we will have the option not to provide our Services, for the provision of which, the consent to use the data was sought.

You can choose to modify your cookie settings of your browser to warn you when a cookie is being sent or to remove or reject cookies. If you choose to remove or reject cookies, it will affect many features or Services on our Website.

Data Sharing

To some extent, data has to be passed on to the courier companies, credit card processing companies, vendors, etc. to enable them to perform their functions related to your order fulfilment.

Apart from this normal business requirement, data may also be disclosed, intercepted, stored, or monitored without your knowledge, with law authorities, for fraud detection, for securing our systems, for enforcing our own rights, for the safety of our Website, employees, management, users, and other affiliates associated with us. In these cases, we will not be liable to you or any third party for any damages howsoever arising from such interception, disclosure, monitoring and storage. This monitoring may include (without limitation) the filtering of incoming and outgoing electronic data messages to identify, limit and/or prevent the transmission of spam, viruses and/or unlawful, defamatory, obscene or otherwise undesirable material or content.

Links to other Sites

Our Website links to other websites, or social plugins that may collect personally identifiable data about you. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of those linked websites.

Data Usage

The most important usage of the data collected from you is your email address which is used to inform you that your order has been confirmed/executed. Your email address is also used to inform you for your customer service-related queries and for any newsletters sent. All other data collected is confidentially stored and will not be disclosed unless needed as per the requirement of the law authorities or in case of any dispute.

Certain data collected from you like your contact data and demographic data may be used to tailor your experience at our Website, providing you with content that we think you might be interested in and to display content according to your preferences.

Data Retention

Your data will only be retained for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was obtained. As registered users, at a minimum this will be for the purpose of providing you Services and as long as your account is active, and likely longer depending upon applicable laws.

If you close your account, we may still retain certain data associated with your account for analytical purposes, record keeping requirements, to prevent fraud, collect fees owed, enforce our rights, or otherwise required by law.

Your Consent

By using the Website and/or by providing your data, you consent to the collection and use of the data you disclose on the Website in accordance with this Privacy Policy, including but not limited to Your consent for sharing your data as per this Privacy Policy.
